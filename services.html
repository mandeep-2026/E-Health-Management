Our Services

Hospital services include everything from basic health care to training and research for major medical school centre.

As well as services created by a network of industry-owned institutions such as health maintenance organisations.

  • Emergency services: Hospitals have emergency departments to handle urgent health issues, such as accidents, fires, and medical emergencies


  • Outpatient services: Hospitals offer outpatient services, such as dentistry, behavioral health services, and rehabilitation services


  • Specialty departments: Hospitals have medical specialty departments, such as intensive care units, operating theaters, and radiology


  • Research and education: Hospitals are often centers for research and teaching Community outreach: Hospitals may participate in community outreach Home and community nursing: Hospitals may offer home and community nursing services


  • Patient communication: Hospitals may provide patient communication services Hospitals can be general hospitals, district hospitals, or "bedless hospitals": General hospitals: Usually have emergency departments
